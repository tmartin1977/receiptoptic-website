Turn paper clutter into smart tax savings

ReceiptOptic is an offline-first financial ledger. Parse receipts with 100% offline signature overrides, map items to Schedule C tax categories, and visualize spending with thick flat 2D charts.

Packed with Smart Utilities

ReceiptOptic runs locally on your PC and mobile devices, bringing lightning-fast analytics to your personal and business finance ledger.

🔒

100% Offline Accuracy

Features zero-config instant parsing via local overrides. Receipt signature matching secures 100% accuracy for standard vendors, working completely without network connection.

⚖️

IRS Schedule C Mappings

Automatically map business expenses to Schedule C tax lines. View YTD tax summaries directly inside your dashboard and export audit-ready receipt archives with one click.

📊

Thick Flat 2D Donut Charts

Track your YTD spending with our upgraded circular visualization. Features 150% thicker arcs, rounded segment caps, and clear side-aligned legend indicators.

👤

Personal Profile Settings

Customize tax profiles with your name, SSN/EIN, business name, custom logo, and business type for personalized, ready-to-file expense exports.
