Turn paper clutter into smart tax savings

ReceiptOptic is an offline-first financial ledger. Parse receipts with 100% offline signature overrides, map items to Schedule C tax categories, and visualize spending with thick flat 2D charts.

💻 Download for PC ⚡ Try Interactive Demo

Packed with Smart Utilities

ReceiptOptic runs locally on your PC and mobile devices, bringing lightning-fast analytics to your personal and business finance ledger.

🔒

100% Offline Accuracy

Zero-config instant parsing via local overrides. Vendor signature matching secures 100% accuracy, working completely without a network connection.

⚖️

IRS Schedule C Mappings

Automatically map business expenses to Schedule C tax lines. View YTD tax summaries and export audit-ready receipt archives with one click.

🤖

Gemini AI OCR Scanner

Connect your Gemini API key to parse complex or crumpled receipts. Instantly detects line items, tax amounts, and payment methods with high-precision AI.

📅

Warranty & Return Tracker

Keep track of active warranties and return periods. Receive notifications before warranties expire and never miss a return deadline again.

☁️

Secure Cloud Backup

Sync your local SQLite database securely via Supabase. Keeps your records backed up in the cloud and synced across your PC and mobile devices.

👤

Profile & Security Lock

Customize tax profiles with your business logo and details. Secure your local finance database with an optional local passcode lock screen.

A Unified Financial Command Center

ReceiptOptic gives you a command center for budget tracking. Manage personal info profile settings, YTD tax summaries, and budget goals inside a modern, private workspace.

🎨 Flat 2D Chart Visualizations

Beautiful thick circular ring segments with rounded caps for spending allocation charts.

💼 IRS-Ready Records

Export database backups and ZIP ledger archives directly to your local storage or flash drives.
